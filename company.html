About Us


OUR STORY

Robotics and automation is taking over the world. In near future, non-skilled jobs lacking in 'human contribution' will be replaced by automation. We started as a distributor of autonomous mobile robots. We failed to get a positive response despite of putting a lot of efforts. Upon analysis we realized that the robots were too costly as compared to the value they provide. This laid the foundation of Efficient Limited.


OUR VISON

To be the creators of low-cost and efficient robots

OUR MISSION

To automate the non-skilled jobs with the ultimate goal to increase efficiency
